Dressing according to the weather is important

Two main criteria when choosing running clothes: You need to stay warm and warm. Both are fraught with consequences both for the body and for the further training process.

Therefore, do not take this issue lightly.

Do not run in a cotton T-shirt and shorts, it is better to choose special running clothes made of synthetic materials. Synthetics wick moisture well and save the body from both hypothermia and overheating.

Summer

Shorts and a T-shirt (T-shirt), that is, short legs and sleeves. Following the "plus 10 degrees" rule, you can comfortably run until it gets colder outside to +15 °C. Don't forget to wear a hat if you run in the afternoon or in the morning.

Spring and autumn

In the weather from +5 to +10 ° C, it is better to choose a long-sleeve T-shirt, as well as trousers or T-shirts. If the temperature is below 0 ° C, it is better to wear insulated trousers. A windbreaker can be useful in rainy weather. At the same time, it is better not to practice in a raincoat: it does not remove sweat, it will have a sauna effect.

Winter

Running clothes should consist of three layers: thermal underwear, a fleece jacket and a light jacket. You will also need a light hat, gloves, and insulated T-shirts. In severe frost, you can wear a mask, scarf or buff. For winter running, you will need special shoes: warm and light enough.
